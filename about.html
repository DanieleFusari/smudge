About Smudge

Smudge is a homemade range of products that focus on being natural and chemical free. As a vegan I have always been passionate about animal free and cruelty free products however after experiencing some health issues I realised the importance of using products with no harmful chemicals. It didn't take long to realise that these types of products were expensive consequently inspiring me to create my own.

I want my products to be natural, chemical free, vegan, organic and affordable. They are simple meaning they are made with relatively few raw ingredients and all used by myself, family and friends.

Testing

No products have been independently tested or trialled for safety or allergens. They are all used by myself, family and friends. Smudge takes no responsibility for any unwanted reactions therefore products used are at the buyers' responsibility. If any unwanted reactions occur, please seek medical advice and cease usage. If products enter the eye area, wash with warm water and seek medical advice.

Delivery

Products will be sent by standard delivery within 3 working days of payment.

Containers

Smudge recycles containers to hold products. These are washed thoroughly but not sterilised. Containers used can be at the buyers' choice e.g. buyers' recycled container.
